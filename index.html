Our Story

Where luxury meets street culture

Founded on Passion

The dynamic portfolio Saint Ventura ranges within the aspects of clothing, entertainment, and culture with a mission to sell exceptional, long-lasting, and high-quality urban wear, through revolutionising urban wear creativity with custom designs into products for customers.

Founded in 2023, Every piece is a relic of exclusivity-high quality, limited edition, never mass-produced. Each garment is more than fabric; it is ambition stitched into seams, resilience embroidered into threads, and destiny woven into its core.

Saint Ventura is for those who treat the streets as their runway, those who choose individuality over conformity, and those who know that fortune favors the rare.

Return Conditions

What We Accept

  • Items in original, unworn condition
  • Items returned within 30 days of purchase
  • Items in original packaging (if applicable)
  • Accessories in pristine condition
  • Items with proof of purchase (order confirmation)

What We Don't Accept

  • Items worn, washed, or altered in any way
  • Items returned after 30-day window
  • Items with stains, odors, or makeup marks
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items purchased with promotional codes (sale items)
  • Items without proof of purchase
  • Undergarments and intimate apparel (hygiene reasons)

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and improving our services.

This Cookie Policy explains how Saint Ventura uses cookies and similar technologies on our website saintventura.co.za.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Shopping cart functionality
  • User authentication and security
  • Payment processing
  • Website navigation and basic features

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Page views and traffic sources
  • Time spent on pages
  • Popular products and content
  • Error tracking and website optimization

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your browsing experience:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Recently viewed products
  • Personalized content and recommendations
  • Chat and customer service features

Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us deliver relevant advertising:

  • Targeted advertisements
  • Social media integration
  • Email marketing optimization
  • Conversion tracking

Third-Party Cookies

We work with trusted partners who may set cookies on our website:

Analytics Providers

  • Google Analytics - website traffic analysis
  • Performance monitoring tools
  • User behavior tracking

Payment Processors

  • Yoco - secure payment processing
  • Credit card processors
  • Fraud prevention services

Social Media Platforms

  • Instagram, TikTok, Google integration
  • Social sharing buttons
  • Social media advertising

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience. Some features of our website may not function properly without cookies, including shopping cart functionality and personalized content.

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years)
  • Essential Cookies: Remain as long as necessary for website function
